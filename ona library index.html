Discover automations, commands, and prompts to supercharge your development workflow with Ona.

Commands are shortcuts you type in Ona (e.g., /review) to instantly run predefined prompts that codify your organization's best practices. Skills are SKILL.md files containing step-by-step workflow instructions. They enable you to teach Ona Agent best practices for specific workflows that matter to you—creating PRs, deploying to staging, writing tests in a particular style. Create your own.

Automations are end-to-end tasks that can be automated through our agent functionality, and enable engineer teams to repeatedly automate tasks of any size. Automations trigger on events (security alerts, pull requests, etc.) or can be manually scheduled. Use one of the Automation templates below, or create your own.
